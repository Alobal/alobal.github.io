$$(cotx)’ = -csc^2x=- rac{1}{sin^2x}$$

$$(secx)’ = secx tanx= rac{sinx}{cos^2x}$$

$$(cscx)’ =-cscxcotx=- rac{ cosx}{sin^2x}$$

$$(arcsinx)’ = rac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$$

$$(arccosx)’ =- rac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$$

$$(arctanx)’ = rac{1}{1+x^2}$$

$$(arccotx)’ =- rac{1}{1+x^2}$$
